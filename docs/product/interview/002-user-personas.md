# 002 - User Personas

## Primary persona — Senior Backend Engineer

**Goals**

- Pass interviews at international companies.
- Improve technical English.
- Practice realistic interviews.

**Pain points**

- Doesn't know their weak areas.
- Gets no structured feedback.
- Doesn't know what to study next.

**Needs**

- Honest AI feedback.
- Personalized recommendations.
- Progress tracking over time.

## Secondary persona — Mid-level dev switching stacks

- Strong in one area, ramping on another (e.g. Node.js dev learning Go).
- Wants targeted practice on the new stack and confidence before applying.

## Success

The user finishes an interview knowing:

- What went well.
- What needs improvement.
- What to practice next (a concrete recommendation, not just a score).
