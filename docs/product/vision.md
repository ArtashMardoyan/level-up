# Vision

> **Derived from: Product Model §1 (What is Level Up), §2 (Why does it exist), §10 (Long-term vision).**

This document organizes the platform's mission and long-term direction. It introduces
no concepts beyond the [Product Model](PRODUCT_MODEL.md); read that first.

## Mission

Make **deliberate interview practice with instant, expert-level coaching repeatable and
accessible**, so improvement is a loop an engineer can actually run — not a hope
(§2). Level Up is a **practice-first, AI-powered platform where software engineers
become interview-ready by rehearsing with an AI coach** (§1).

## Why it exists

Engineers fail interviews less from lack of knowledge than from lack of realistic
practice, immediate feedback, and — for many — communication skill. The status quo
(static question lists, passive video courses) offers no rehearsal, no expert feedback,
no personalization, no measure of progress. Level Up closes that gap. See §2.

## Long-term vision (≈ 3 years)

Level Up grows from "practice interviews with an AI" into a **personalized, multi-modal
learning ecosystem** that knows each user and coaches them toward career growth. The
milestones — realtime voice AI interviews, adaptive personalized learning, full
English-communication coaching, Mobile, and company/role-specific modes — are defined in
§10 and sequenced in [`roadmap.md`](roadmap.md).
